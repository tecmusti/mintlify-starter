estudjo is an AI platform that transforms a single product photo into polished marketing images and videos. Use the REST API to integrate AI generation directly into your workflows, automation tools, or applications. Whether you're building an e-commerce pipeline or a creative automation tool, estudjo gives you studio-quality output from a single image URL.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your API key">
    Sign in to the estudjo dashboard at [estudjo.com](https://www.estudjo.com) and generate an API key from your account settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Queue a generation">
    Send a `POST /generate` request with your product image URL and a scene identifier. You'll receive a `task_id` in the response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Poll for results">
    Call `GET /status` with your `task_id` until the `status` field reads `completed`, then download your outputs from the `result_urls` array.
  </Step>
</Steps>
